Cyclescheme

Fictional Cycles Ltd are a partner of Cyclescheme, the UK's number one provider of tax-free bikes for the Government's Cycle to Work initiative.

How does it work?

Simply select the bike and equipment that you want then we will provide you with a paper quote. You can then use Cyclescheme's online tool to apply for your chosen package by entering the details online and requesting a secure voucher which is then approved by your employer. Once payment has been setup to be deducted from your gross monthly salary, you can come in store with photo ID and collect your bike.
